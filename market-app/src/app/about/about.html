About Esfam Market

ESFAM Benin University, established in 2008, is a non-profit private higher education institution situated in the city of Porto Novo within the Oueme region. With a population range of 250,000 to 499,999 inhabitants, the university is officially accredited and recognized by the Ministère de l’Enseignement Supérieur et de la Recherche Scientifique, Benin (Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research, Benin). Offering coeducational programs leading to various officially recognized higher education degrees, including bachelor's degrees across multiple fields of study, ESFAM Benin University is renowned for its commitment to academic excellence and holds high esteem from the Ministry of Higher Education, Benin Republic.
